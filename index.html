About Canux Corporation

and our Business Enhancement successes

Canux Corporation is a boutique management consultancy, located in Victoria, British Columbia, focused on delivering high-value business enhancement services to our customers across Canada and the U.S. With over 14 years of experience in business development, Canux customers have:

  • generated more than $170 million in new revenue through new products and services
  • reduced "time on task" by more than 30% through process improvement exercises
  • reduced time to market for new product initiatives by as much as 50%
  • saved millions in on-going expenses.

Through careful analysis and streamlined process improvement, our customers have realized consistency in deliverables, increased project completion rates, and reduced the chaos and clutter of documenting projects.

What do we do?

Canux works with your executive management to align corporate goals with strategic initiatives to ensure your business will achieve its maximum potential.

Canux Corporation was founded in 2003 to provide business-to-business and business-to-consumer solutions to an underserved Canadian marketplace. Over the years Canux has grown into a strategic business partner - helping our clients achieve business success through the delivery of our business enhancement services.

Canux Corporation consists of a team of industry veterans who we refer to as “Business Enhancement Agents.”

Our Business Enhancement Agents are skilled in a wide variety of approaches, ontologies and frameworks such as TOGAF, LEAN Six Sigma, PMP, ITIL, FMEA, and many others. Collectively, they have produced innovative solutions and industry research for companies across North America such as GE, Microsoft, CyrusOne, Norbord, Cincinnati Bell, BMW Financial Solutions, E.W. Scripps Co., Alcatel-Lucent, as well as many other private and public sector organizations, large and small.

Often, the road to transforming business can take different methods, all of which can be addressed by our consultants. With their decades of experience, they work hard to enhance your lines of business, maximize your success potential, and implement successful transformational exercises to help your company get the results you desire. Our agents, once assigned to you, are with you from cradle to grave on your projects. They mentor your teams on new services and practices, they help your sales team win new business, and they help improve the overall efficiency of your company.

Canux begins with a conversation to understand what your business does at its core and what your expectations are for the future. Are you ready to schedule a time to chat with us?
